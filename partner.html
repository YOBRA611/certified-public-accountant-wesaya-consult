Industries We Serve

Overview

We proudly serve a diverse portfolio of clients across Kenya’s dynamic economic landscape, regional and globally. Our industry-focused approach allows us to understand sector-specific regulations, reporting requirements, and operational challenges unique to the Kenyan environment.

With deep knowledge of compliance frameworks such as the Kenya Revenue Authority (KRA) regulations, the NGO Coordination Board, the Public Benefit Organizations (PBO) Act, Central Bank of Kenya (CBK) guidelines, and other statutory requirements, we help our clients operate confidently, compliantly, and sustainably.

Who we serve

Non-Governmental Organizations (NGOs)

Non-Governmental Organizations in Kenya operate under strict oversight from the NGO Coordination Board and are often accountable to multiple stakeholders including donors, development partners, and government agencies.

We support NGOs with:

  • Donor fund accountability and grant reporting
  • Compliance with NGO Board requirements
  • Financial statement preparation in accordance with applicable standards
  • Internal audits and risk management reviews
  • Budget monitoring and expenditure tracking
  • KRA tax compliance, including PAYE, VAT (where applicable), and withholding tax

Our services are designed to ensure transparency, strengthen governance structures, and enhance donor confidence. We help NGOs maintain clean financial records and meet both statutory and donor reporting deadlines without stress.

Non-Profit Organizations (NPOs)

Non-Profit Organizations, including charities, foundations, and faith-based organizations, require strong financial oversight to ensure sustainability and regulatory compliance.

We assist NPOs with:

  • Registration and compliance advisory
  • Financial reporting and bookkeeping systems
  • Governance and internal control structures
  • Tax exemption advisory and KRA compliance
  • Annual returns and statutory filings

Our role is to help nonprofit entities demonstrate accountability, maintain public trust, and ensure proper utilization of funds in alignment with their mission and legal obligations.

Micro, Small & Medium Enterprises (MSMEs)

MSMEs are the backbone of global economy, contributing significantly to employment and GDP growth. However, many small and growing businesses struggle with financial management, tax compliance, and regulatory requirements.

We support MSMEs with:

  • Bookkeeping and accounting system setup
  • KRA compliance (iTax filings, VAT, PAYE, corporate tax, turnover tax)
  • Financial statement preparation
  • Cash flow planning and budgeting
  • Business advisory and growth planning
  • Tax planning and audit support

We simplify compliance so business owners can focus on growth and operations. Our practical, hands-on approach ensures MSMEs remain compliant while building financially sound and scalable enterprises.

Financial Institutions

Financial institutions operate in a highly regulated environment governed by authorities such as the Central Bank of Kenya (CBK), SACCO Societies Regulatory Authority (SASRA), and other regulatory bodies.

We provide specialized services including:

  • Internal and external audit support
  • Regulatory compliance reviews
  • Risk management and internal control assessments
  • Financial reporting advisory
  • Corporate governance advisory

Our team understands the complexity of regulatory reporting and risk management within Kenya’s financial sector. We help institutions strengthen compliance frameworks, improve reporting accuracy, and enhance operational resilience.

Individuals

We also serve individuals seeking professional financial and tax guidance within Kenya’s and global evolving regulatory environment.

Our services include:

  • Personal income tax filing through KRA iTax
  • Tax planning and advisory
  • Wealth planning and investment advisory
  • Capital gains tax advisory
  • Rental income tax compliance
  • Tax dispute support

We provide clear, practical advice to help individuals remain compliant while optimizing their financial position. Whether you are an entrepreneur, professional, landlord, or investor, we help you navigate financial obligations with confidence.
